To minimize risk of general safety tips

  • The garage should be off limits to children this age at all times. Install child-resistant door handle covers so children are not able to get into the garage.
  • Make sure power tools are unplugged and locked in a childproof cabinet when not in use. Remove the blades and bits before storing.
  • Lock guns and ammunition in a secured cabinet. Out of reach and out of site of children.
  • Use only garage door openers with automatic stopping devices.
