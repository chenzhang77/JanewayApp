To minimize risk of burns

  • Cap all electrical outlets with childproof covers.
  • If you have a fireplace install a screen to reduce the risk of burns. Also keep fireplace tools out of reach of children.
  • Install both smoke and carbon monoxide detectors. Test them monthly and change the batteries at least once a year.
  • Never leave a child alone in a room with a burning candle.
  • Keep candles out of reach of children.
  • Remove any lighters or matches and keep out of reach of children.
