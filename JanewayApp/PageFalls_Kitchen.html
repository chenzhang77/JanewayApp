To minimize risk of Falls

  • Wipe up spills on floor so your child does not slip and fall.
  • Keep cords for smaller electrical appliances out of reach from your child; they may injure or burn themselves if they pull them off of the counter onto themselves.
  • Make sure all major appliances such as fridge and stove; are properly grounded.
  • Install protective cushioning where possible in areas with sharp edges and corners.
