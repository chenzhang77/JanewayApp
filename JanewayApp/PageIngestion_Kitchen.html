To minimize risk of ingestion

  • Laundry packets are extremely toxic and should be used with caution around children. Keep them in a locked cupboard and try to do laundry when your children are not around you.
  • Keep all cleaning products and harmful household products locked and away from children.
  • Keep products in their original containers so everyone knows what they are.
  • When poisonous products cannot be locked keep them up high out of reach of children.
  • Install safety latches, locks and doorknob covers on all cupboards, closets and doors when poisonous products are kept.
