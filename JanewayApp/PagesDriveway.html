• Pick up any toys and play items that can attract your child from the driveway.
  • When possible back into your driveway so that you can pull forward. If you must back out of your driveway, check all mirrors carefully or do a walk around your car before to make sure there are no children around.
  • Make sure walkways and stairwell are well lit and free of toys or objects blocking a clear path.
  • Keep all outdoor toys in a dry and secure place when not in use.
  • Inspect front and backyards regularly for glass, metal and sharp objects.
  • Empty water containers such as large pails and wading pools. Children can drown in as little as an inch of water.
  • Make sure all garbage bins are covered and secured.
