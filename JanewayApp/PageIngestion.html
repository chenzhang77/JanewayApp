To minimize risk of ingestion or choking

  • Install child proof safety latches on cupboards and drawers.
  • Make sure all prescription medications, cosmetics and household cleaners are locked and out of reach from your child.
  • Mouthwash, nail polish, perfume and hair spray must be locked in a cabinet or cupboard away from your child.
  • Have razor blades, scissors, nail clippers and all sharp tools locked in a cabinet or cupboard.
  • Do not use toilet disinfectant.
