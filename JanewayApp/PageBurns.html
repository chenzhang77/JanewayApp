To minimize risk of burning

  • Cover the spout on the bathtub and teach children not to touch the spout.
  • Test the temperature of the water with your wrist, elbow or temperature-sensing toy before placing your child in the tub.
  • Set hot water heater to no more than 49 Celsius or have an anti-scald device installed on faucets.
  • Cap all electrical outlets with covers.
  • Always unplug small appliances such as a hair dryer and straightening/curling iron.
