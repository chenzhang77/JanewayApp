To minimize risk from Highchairs

  • A chair should be stable and have a wide base to reduce the risk of tipping.
  • There should be a restraint system that consists of a 3- or 5- point harness system that should fit snug and keep your child in place.
  • If using a booster seat, make sure your child has proper coordination to sit up properly in one and use all straps to secure your child.
