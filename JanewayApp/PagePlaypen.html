To minimize risk of playpen

  • Never tie a pacifier or other items around a childâ€™s neck or affix a cord to clothing. Children have fallen in playpens and caused serious injury because a cord or string was caught on the playpen.
  • Playpens are not intended to be used for unsupervised sleep; they do not meet the same safety regulations as cribs.
  • Do not add blankets, pillows, an extra mattress or stuffed toys to playpens.
  • Supervise a child in a playpen at all times.
  • Do not place a playpen near doors or other furniture.
  • Do not place large stuffed animals in the playpen, your child could use them to climb out.
