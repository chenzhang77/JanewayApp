To minimize risk of Crib safety

  • Do not use a crib made before 1986, as it does not meet current regulations.
  • Always follow the manufacturerâ€™s instructions for putting together and using the crib.
  • Avoid the use of loose bedding and soft objects such as blankets, quilts, soft toys and pillows
  • Place the mattress in the lowest position once your child can push up on his/her hands and knees and remove mobiles as well at this time.
  • Never place a crib near a window. If you do make sure drapery and blind cords are out of reach to prevent strangulation.
